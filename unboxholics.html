Unboxholics is a Greek-based media company that focuses on video games, technology, and entertainment content. They were founded in 2012 by a group of friends who shared a passion for video games and wanted to create a community where people could come together and share their love for gaming. The company's main platform is their YouTube channel, which has over 1.5 million subscribers and features a wide range of video game content, including game reviews, walkthroughs, and news. They also have a website and social media accounts where they share additional content and interact with their fans.
